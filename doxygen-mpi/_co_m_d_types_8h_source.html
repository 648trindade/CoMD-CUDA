16 /// The base struct from which all potentials derive. Think of this as an
17 /// abstract base class.
18 ///
19 /// CoMD uses the following units:
20 /// - distance is in Angstroms
21 /// - energy is in eV
22 /// - time is in fs
23 /// - force in in eV/Angstrom
24 ///
25 /// The choice of distance, energy, and time units means that the unit
26 /// of mass is eV*fs^2/Angstrom^2. Hence, we must convert masses that
27 /// are input in AMU (atomic mass units) into internal mass units.
28 typedef struct BasePotentialSt
29 {
30  real_t cutoff; //!< potential cutoff distance in Angstroms
